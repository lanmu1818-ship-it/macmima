# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want MacMima to be a respectful and useful project for developers and teams.
Participants are expected to communicate with care, assume good intent, and keep
discussions focused on the work.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Publishing another person's private information
- Intentionally disruptive comments or spam
- Public disclosure of unpatched security vulnerabilities

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when
behavior harms the project or community.
